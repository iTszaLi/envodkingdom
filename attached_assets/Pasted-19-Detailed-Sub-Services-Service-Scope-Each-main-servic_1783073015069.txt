19. Detailed Sub-Services & Service Scope

Each main service page must include a comprehensive list of sub-services and capabilities. These should be displayed as premium cards, expandable sections, or an interactive grid. Every sub-service should have a short description and a "Request This Service" button that opens a pre-filled email to info@envodkingdom.net.
