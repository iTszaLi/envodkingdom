The new Global Operations Network section looks much cleaner and more professional. However, there are still a few improvements needed to make it feel polished and enterprise-grade.

1. Add Country Flags

The office cards currently only display country abbreviations (SA, AE, BH, IN). Replace or accompany these abbreviations with high-quality circular SVG country flags.

Examples:

🇸🇦 Saudi Arabia
🇦🇪 United Arab Emirates
🇧🇭 Bahrain
🇮🇳 India

Also add the country flag inside the office information panel next to the country name.

The flags should be:

Small (20–24px)
Crisp SVGs
Consistent size
Properly aligned with the text
2. Improve the Office Information Panel

The right-hand information panel feels too empty.

Enhance it by adding:

Country flag beside the country name
Office type (Head Office / Branch)
Office address
Business hours
Phone number
Email
CTA button:
View on Google Maps
Contact this Office

This will make the panel more useful and balanced.

3. Improve the Office Cards

Each office card should include:

Country flag
Office name
Country
Office type
Online status
3–4 service icons

Increase spacing and improve alignment so everything feels balanced.

4. Map Improvements
Make office markers slightly larger.
Highlight the selected office more clearly.
Dim the other offices when one is selected.
Keep the route lines subtle.
Remove unnecessary visual clutter.

The map should remain simple and professional.

5. Add a Small Legend

In the bottom-left corner of the map, improve the legend by showing:

🔴 Head Office

⚪ Branch Office

➖ Logistics Network

Use cleaner icons and typography.

6. Better Hover Interaction

Hovering over an office card should:

Highlight the corresponding office marker.
Open/update the office details panel.
Slightly brighten only the relevant connection route.
Add a subtle lift effect to the selected office card.

No excessive animations.

7. Typography & Spacing
Increase padding inside the information panel.
Improve spacing between sections.
Make section headings slightly bolder.
Align icons perfectly with text.
Ensure all cards have consistent heights.
8. Enterprise Polish

The entire section should feel like a logistics dashboard used by companies such as DHL, Maersk, DP World, DSV, FedEx, and Kuehne+Nagel.

Focus on:

Clean layout
Premium typography
Consistent spacing
Professional icons
Minimal animations
Excellent responsiveness
High-quality SVG assets

The goal is to create a section that immediately communicates trust, global presence, and operational excellence without looking overly decorative or cluttered.
